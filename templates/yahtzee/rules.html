🎲 Yahtzee Rules & Tips

Master the classic dice game!

📋 Game Overview

Yahtzee is a dice game where players roll five dice to make specific combinations. The goal is to score the most points by rolling different combinations over 13 rounds.

Players: 1-6 players
Rounds: 13 rounds per game
Rolls per turn: Up to 3 rolls

🎯 How to Play

Turn Structure

  1. First Roll: Roll all 5 dice
  2. Keep or Reroll: Choose which dice to keep and which to reroll (up to 2 more times)
  3. Score: After your third roll (or earlier if satisfied), choose a category to score in
  4. Next Player: Play passes to the next player

Keeping Dice

Between rolls, select dice you want to keep by checking the boxes. Unchecked dice will be rerolled. You can change which dice to keep between each roll.

💡 Tip:
You don't have to use all 3 rolls! If you're happy with your dice after roll 1 or 2, you can score immediately.

📊 Scoring Categories

Upper Section (Simple)

Score the sum of dice showing that number:

Category Description Example Score
Ones Sum of all 1s 1-1-3-1-5 3 points
Twos Sum of all 2s 2-2-4-2-6 6 points
Threes Sum of all 3s 3-3-3-5-1 9 points
Fours Sum of all 4s 4-4-4-4-2 16 points
Fives Sum of all 5s 5-5-6-5-3 15 points
Sixes Sum of all 6s 6-6-6-2-3 18 points
🎁 Upper Section Bonus:
If your upper section total is 63 or more, you get a 35-point bonus! (That's an average of 3 per category)

Lower Section (Combinations)

Category Description Example Score
Three of a Kind At least 3 dice the same 4-4-4-2-6 Sum of all dice (20)
Four of a Kind At least 4 dice the same 5-5-5-5-3 Sum of all dice (23)
Full House 3 of one number + 2 of another 3-3-3-6-6 25 points
Small Straight 4 consecutive numbers 1-2-3-4-6 or 2-3-4-5-1 30 points
Large Straight 5 consecutive numbers 1-2-3-4-5 or 2-3-4-5-6 40 points
Yahtzee All 5 dice the same 6-6-6-6-6 50 points
Chance Any combination 1-3-4-5-6 Sum of all dice (19)
🎉 Yahtzee Bonus:
If you score a second Yahtzee (after already scoring 50 in the Yahtzee box), you get an extra 100 points!

🧠 Strategy Tips

🎯 Upper Section Strategy
  • Aim for at least 3 of each number (3×1=3, 3×2=6, 3×3=9, etc.)
  • Focus on high numbers (4s, 5s, 6s) early for easier bonus
  • Don't waste good rolls on low-value upper categories
🎲 Lower Section Strategy
  • Yahtzee (50 pts): Rare! Don't chase it unless you have 3-4 matching dice
  • Large Straight (40 pts): Hard to get, but valuable
  • Full House (25 pts): Look for pairs and three-of-a-kinds
  • Chance: Use as a backup for poor rolls, especially high totals
🎖️ General Strategy
  • Keep your options open early in the game
  • Use "Chance" or low upper categories as throwaway boxes for bad rolls
  • Focus on completing the upper section bonus (63+ points)
  • After your first roll, decide on a target category and keep relevant dice
  • In the late game, take safe points rather than gambling
  • If forced to take a zero, use it on a category you're unlikely to complete well
📈 Probability Tips
  • With 2 rolls left, the chance of getting a specific number is about 75%
  • Small straights are more common than you think (look for 4 in a row)
  • Three of a kind with 3 dice kept gives ~50% chance of getting four of a kind
  • If you have 4 matching dice, go for Yahtzee—you have a 1/6 chance!

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Chasing Yahtzee too early: Don't waste turns trying for 50 points when you could secure other points
  • Ignoring the upper bonus: That 35-point bonus is huge!
  • Filling high-scoring categories early: Save them for when you actually have good rolls
  • Not using Chance wisely: It's a great safety net for high-value poor rolls
  • Keeping too many dice: Sometimes you need to break up a pair to go for a straight

🏆 Winning the Game

After all players complete 13 rounds, total up:

  • Upper section scores
  • Upper section bonus (35 points if total ≥ 63)
  • Lower section scores
  • Yahtzee bonuses (100 points each)

The player with the highest total wins!

🎯 Average Scores:
  • Beginner: 150-200 points
  • Intermediate: 200-250 points
  • Expert: 250-300+ points
